Juicy Chicken Burger

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breast fillets
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Mayonnaise or burger sauce
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breast fillets in half horizontally to make thinner pieces.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken piece into the beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the chicken pieces until golden and cooked through.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly in a pan or toaster.
  6. Assemble the burger: Spread mayonnaise or burger sauce on the bun base, add lettuce, a slice of fried chicken, tomato slices, and a cheese slice. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your juicy chicken burger!
