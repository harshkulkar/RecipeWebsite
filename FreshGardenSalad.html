Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens thoroughly.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the greens, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and shredded carrots.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients evenly.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your light and refreshing Fresh Garden Salad!
