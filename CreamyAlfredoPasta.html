Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Stir continuously.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and mix until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the Alfredo sauce.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm.
