Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the top and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  7. Remove from the oven, add fresh basil leaves, and slice.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
